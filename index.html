OWL

Weight: 1.2 to 3.2kg

Lifespan: Average of 8 to 10years.

Flight distance: Up to 3000 miles.

Location: Everywhere in the world, except Antarctica

Fact:
Owls are adept hunters and nocturnal animals.
Owls share an innate ability to fly silently.
Owls are unable to move their eyes, but they can turn their heads to an angle of 270 degrees.
Owls have specalized auditory functions that help provide acoustic information to locate and pursure their prey.
